MARIACHI DIVINAS DE SAN DIEGO

The world of Mariachi is traditionally known to be dominated by all male groups. This custom held true until recently. During the past 20 years, there has been an uprising to the concept of an all female mariachi band. Bands such as Mariachi Reyes De Los Angeles and Mariachi Divas have appeared on the professional stage. This is a trend that has mainly made its home in the city of Los Angeles. Though as of now, its ideals have spread down to the south bay with the creation of San Diego’s first all female mariachi: Mariachi Divinas.

Through the dedication of Director Dulce Perez, Mariachi Divinas was formed out of the local female mariachi population. Since this population is quite small, finding the right people for the job was quite difficult. Dulce then turned to her school friends for help. As a result, the band was created out of experienced female musicians who had been good friends for years.

Established in 2012, the group is generally still very young. Though they have only been playing as a group for 2 years, their combined musical experience amounts to that of the professionals. San Diego born and raised, Mariachi Divinas sets its sight to contributing to the continuing feminine movement in the world of Mariachi.

DULCE - 1st Violin

As founder and director of Mariachi Divinas, Dulce has dedicated herself to her group. In a difficult search for all female musicals, she has enlisted the help of her long time school friends. Under her guidance, Divinas has grown to be a well-known mariachi in San Diego and beyond.

With over 10 years of musical experience, Dulce is currently studying for her degree in music from Southwestern College. Under the mentorship of the famous Jeff Nevin, she continues to grow as a student and as a musician.

She plays the role of the first violin and is one of the main singers of the group.

JASMINE - 2nd Violin

Jasmine is the second violin for Mariachi Divinas. Having most of her musical experience in classical music, she has adapted to the mariachi style with hard work and practice.

Balancing work and school, Jasmine is a dedicated musician and student. She is currently studying for a degree in music at Southwestern College.

Jasmine plays second violin alongside Dulce. She is another singer in the group and has over 10 years of musical experience.

ANGELA - Guitarron

Angela is one of the rare female guitarron players in San Diego. She is also one of the rare mariachi musicians that doesn’t know Spanish. Despite this she has a passion for the traditional mariachi music and enjoys helping the girls who were desperately in need of a female base.

She is currently studying for a degree in human computer interaction at the University of California San Diego.

She is the base player of the group and provides background vocals. She possess over 10 years of mariachi experience enjoys playing other instruments on occasion.

ABBY - Vihuela

Abby is the groups youngest member. Though not as experienced as the rest of the group, she has developed and improved her musicianship through her experiences with the group.

She is currently studying for a degree in science at Southwestern College.

Abby plays the small vihuela for the group and will sometimes provide background vocals.

VIVIAN - Guitar

Vivan is the energetic member of the group. She is passionate about music and reflects it in her music. She is also the makeup director of the group and is in charge of making the girls look pretty and professional.

She is currently studying at Kaplan College for a degree in medical care. Though she has already graduated from Southwestern College, she will occasionally help the school mariachi. She is also a contestant in the Mexican version of The Voice (El Voz) and is one of the finalists.

She plays guitar and is another one of the main signers for the group. With over 15 years of guitar experience, she is the most experienced member of the group.
